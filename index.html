'gg' 'G'

'gg' will take you to the top of the page

'G' will take you to the bottom of the page

'w' 'W'

'w' will take you to the beginning of the next word

'W' will take you to the beginning of the next word after a whitespace

'w'

'W'

'e' 'E'

'e' will take you to the end of the next word

'E' will take you to the end of the next word after a whitespace

'e'

'E'

'b' 'B'

'b' will take you to the beginning of the previous word

'B' will take you to the beginning of the previous word after a whitespace

'b'

'B'

Thats it ... for now
